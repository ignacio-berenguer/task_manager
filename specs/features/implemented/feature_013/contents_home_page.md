#### Problem Statement

Organizations struggle to manage digital initiatives effectively due to fragmented views, inconsistent evaluation, difficult prioritization, and limited visibility on progress, risks, and value.  
This platform solves these challenges by centralizing the portfolio lifecycle.

---

#### Key Features Overview

- **Portfolio Visualization:** Heatmaps, bubble charts, roadmaps.
- **Intake & Evaluation Workflow:** Standardized forms, scoring models, business case templates.
- **Prioritization Engine:** Value vs. effort, scenario modeling, risk scoring.
- **Financial Tracking:** Budgets, forecasts, benefits, cumulative value.
- **Execution Monitoring:** KPIs/OKRs, milestones, RAG status.
- **Governance Tools:** Stage gates, approvals, audit trails.
- **Collaboration:** Comments, notifications, shared dashboards.
- **Integrations:** Jira, Azure DevOps, Power BI, Teams, etc.

---

#### How It Works (Process Overview)

1. **Capture** – Intake ideas and business cases.
2. **Evaluate** – Score impact, cost, and risk with structured criteria.
3. **Prioritize** – Compare scenarios and align with strategic goals.
4. **Execute** – Monitor delivery, risks, and value realization in real time.
